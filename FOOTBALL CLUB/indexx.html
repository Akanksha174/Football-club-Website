REVIEW OF THE SEASON

A change was plainly needed after the first half of the 2021–22 campaign as it was difficult and the environment turned toxic in Koeman's last months. It is undeniable that the club's Champions League and Europa League campaigns were abject failures, and Xavi has faced some challenging times as manager. However, there are many reasons to be optimistic about the future, and Barcelona will soon be back in the mix, contending for the top honours with the appropriate summer transfer window.
